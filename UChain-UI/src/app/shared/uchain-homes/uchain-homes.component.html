Ethiopia's Coffee & Teff Supply Chain System

About Us

U-Chain is a next-generation supply chain system built to transform Ethiopia's Coffee and Teff market through transparency, technology, and trust.

Vision and Goals

  • Empower farmers and suppliers
  • Connect all stakeholders through a unified platform
  • Promote local trade through smart contracts, real-time tracking, and digital payments
  • Define and protect Ethiopia's agricultural heritage
